Pigs In A Blanket

Description

This pork centered meal comes from every corner of the world. The dough brilliantly compliments the highly processed organs of the swine that companies have no other use for. It's a MUST TRY!

Ingredients

  • 1 (8-oz.) tube crescent rolls
  • 12-oz. package mini cocktail weiners
  • 4 tbsp. melted butter
  • Coarse salt, for sprinkling

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 375ยบ. On a lightly floured surface, unroll crescent sheets and tear where perforated. Cut each triangle into 3 smaller triangles
  2. Place one cocktail weiner on thick side of each triangle then gently roll to thinner side
  3. Transfer to a medium baking sheet, brush with melted butter, and a sprinkle with coarse salt
  4. Bake until golden, 12 to 15 minutes
  5. Bon Appetit!
