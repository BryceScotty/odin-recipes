Deep-Fried Oreos

Description

Out of the 3 recipes, this one is by far the healthiest, especially for any individual who has extremely thick blood, this just just for you! Packed to the nutrition label with vital nutrients and supplements, this dish should be consumed multiple times, every hour of every day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pancake mix
  • 2/3 cup whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 18 Oreo cookies
  • Additional vegetable oil or canola oil for frying

Procedure

  1. Freeze 18 Oreo cookies for about an hour
  2. Fill a heavy cooking pot with 2-3 inches of cooking oil and heat to 375Â°F.
  3. Combine pancake mix, milk, egg, and 2 teaspoons vegetable oil in large bowl and whisk until smooth.
  4. Dip a cookie in the mixture and completely cover it so you can't see through the batter.
  5. Carefully drop the coated cookie(s) in the hot oil and fry on each side just until golden brown.
  6. Remove cookie from oil with tongs and drain on paper towels.
